City

Bethlehem, at the heart of Palestine, is a historically rich and captivating city, famous as the birthplace of Jesus Christ. Its ancient streets tell tales of generations past, and the people uphold traditions with strength. Surrounded by the beautiful West Bank landscapes, it's a calm place with friendly, hospitable residents. Bethlehem holds global significance as a symbol of hope and welcomes travelers from all corners of the world. In this city, history and modern life coexist in the Holy Land.
